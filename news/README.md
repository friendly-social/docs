# News

Each quarter we post news in that folder. The news are not posted often, but regular. These is a deliberate choice to avoid making it a routine thing to maintain.

## Naming

News are formed based on seasons. Year is the year of the _first_ month of the
season and the following digit is a number of that season in the year. Example:

- 2025.1 is Spring of 2025
- 2025.2 is Summer of 2025
- 2025.3 is Autumn of 2025
- 2025.4 is Winter of 2025

Even though Winter is ended in 2026, the number is made by the starting month.
